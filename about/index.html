About Jack Wilder

FROM ATLANTA TO PITTSBURGH

🎸

The Story

Jack Wilder grew up in Atlanta, Georgia, where he was surrounded by the sounds of classic country and southern rock. From an early age, he was captivated by country music, inspired by artists who told authentic stories through their songs. He picked up the guitar at age 12 and never looked back.

Spending his formative years in Atlanta gave Jack a deep appreciation for Southern culture and music traditions. He spent countless hours learning from local musicians, performing at open mics, and developing the songwriting skills that would define his unique sound.

In 2018, Jack made the bold move to Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, seeking new opportunities and a fresh creative environment. The Steel City's thriving music scene and growing country community welcomed him with open arms. Pittsburgh's gritty, authentic spirit resonated deeply with Jack's approach to country music.

Musical Journey

Early Influences

Jack's musical foundation comes from classic country artists like Waylon Jennings, Willie Nelson, and Hank Williams, combined with modern influences like Morgan Wallen, Dan + Shay, and Dylan Marlowe.

The Y108 Moment

In a viral moment on Pittsburgh's Y108 "Song on the Spot" show, Jack wrote an authentic country song about Pittsburgh in just 5 minutes, gaining significant local attention and fan recognition.

Recording & Releases

Jack has released several singles including his recent breakthrough hit "Cowboy Heartbreaker," which showcases his ability to blend classic country storytelling with modern production.

Live Performances

Known for high-energy live shows, Jack regularly performs at venues across Pittsburgh and Pennsylvania, from intimate clubs to festivals, always delivering authentic country performances.

Songwriting

Jack's songwriting focuses on authentic storytelling about relationships, identity, and the human experience, drawing from both personal experiences and observations of life.

Growing Recognition

With thousands of followers across streaming platforms and social media, Jack is building a dedicated fanbase as Pittsburgh's emerging country star.

Sound & Style

Genre: Alt-Country & Pop Country

Jack's sound sits at the intersection of authentic country traditions and contemporary pop sensibilities. He draws inspiration from the narrative-driven songwriting of traditional country while incorporating modern production and melodies that resonate with today's listeners.

His music tackles real emotions and experiences: heartbreak, ambition, identity, and the search for authenticity in an increasingly digital world. Whether it's a stripped-down acoustic ballad or a full band production, Jack's songs connect through honest storytelling.
