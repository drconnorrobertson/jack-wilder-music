Booking Information

Booking Timeline

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for large events. However, we do accept last-minute bookings based on availability. The earlier you book, the more flexibility we have with arrangements.

Technical Requirements

Jack provides professional equipment for most events. For large venues or festivals, we work with your technical team to ensure everything is perfectly set up.

Setlist Customization

All performances include customized setlists tailored to your event. Want specific songs? We can learn special requests with advance notice.

Travel & Logistics

Based in Pittsburgh, we regularly perform throughout Pennsylvania and Ohio. Travel fees apply for events outside the Pittsburgh area.

Payment Terms

Typical terms: 50% deposit to reserve the date, 50% due upon completion. We accept all major payment methods for your convenience.

Cancellation Policy

Deposits are fully refundable if cancelled more than 30 days before the event. For closer dates, we work with clients on a case-by-case basis.
