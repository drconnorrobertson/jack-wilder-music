Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book Jack for an event?

Visit the Booking page and fill out the event inquiry form with your event details. You can also email jackwildermanagement@gmail.com directly. We typically respond within 24-48 hours with pricing and availability.

What is the booking timeline?

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for the best flexibility and availability. However, we do accept last-minute bookings based on schedule. The earlier you book, the more we can customize the performance.

What's included in a booking?

Booking packages include professional performance, customized setlist, and technical setup. Acoustic packages include sound system. Full band packages include sound, lights, and crew. All performances can include special requests with advance notice.

Do you travel outside Pittsburgh?

Yes! Jack regularly performs throughout Pennsylvania and Ohio. Travel fees apply for events outside the Pittsburgh area. We work with event planners nationwide, so don't hesitate to reach out if you're outside our region.

What's your cancellation policy?

Deposits are fully refundable if cancelled more than 30 days before the event. For cancellations within 30 days, we work with clients on a case-by-case basis. We understand life happens, and we're flexible with our clients.

How do I get press materials?

Visit the Press Kit page for high-resolution photos, official biography, music links, and media information. All materials are available for download. For additional press needs, contact jackwildermanagement@gmail.com.

Still have questions?

Use the contact form above or email jackwildermanagement@gmail.com. We're here to help and would love to hear from you.
