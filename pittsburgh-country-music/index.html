Country Music Comes to Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh has a rich musical heritage, from rock and roll to hip-hop, but one genre has been gaining momentum in Steel City: country music. Jack Wilder is at the forefront of Pittsburgh's growing country music scene, bringing authentic alt-country and pop country to venues, festivals, and events throughout the region.

Why Jack Wilder?

As a Pittsburgh-based artist, Jack understands the city's culture and character. His music speaks to the experiences of Steel City residents while maintaining the authenticity and storytelling that define great country music. Whether you're a longtime country fan or new to the genre, Jack's music has something for everyone.

Available for All Events

From intimate acoustic sets to full band performances, Jack is available for weddings, corporate events, private parties, festivals, and venue performances throughout Pittsburgh and beyond.

Pittsburgh's country music scene is experiencing a renaissance. With venues opening their stages to country acts and audiences actively seeking out live country performances, Steel City is becoming a destination for authentic country music.
