About Jack

Jack Wilder is a Pittsburgh-based country music artist who grew up in Atlanta, Georgia, where he developed his passion for songwriting and performing. His blend of alt-country and pop country has resonated with audiences across the region.

Known for his authentic sound and dynamic live performances, Jack draws inspiration from artists like Morgan Wallen, Dan + Shay, and Dylan Marlowe. He recently gained attention for writing a Pittsburgh country song in just 5 minutes on Y108's "Song on the Spot" radio show.

With his recent release "Cowboy Heartbreaker," Jack continues to build momentum as one of Pittsburgh's most exciting emerging country artists.
