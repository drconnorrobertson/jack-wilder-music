About Jack

Hey there! I'm Jack Wilder. Pop country music artist based in Pittsburgh, PA but I travel all over! Me and my gang love to have fun so, if you hate fun... don't book me. But if you wanna have the best time at your event I got your back!

If you like artists like Morgan Wallen, Dylan Marlowe, and Dan + Shay, then you'll love our live show! I recently gained attention for writing a Pittsburgh country song in just 5 minutes on Y108's "Song on the Spot" radio show.

Pricing: $400-$1,000 per event. Available for booking up to 200 miles from Pittsburgh.
