Official Biography

Jack Wilder - Pittsburgh Country Music Artist

Hey there! I'm Jack Wilder. I'm a pop country music artist based in Pittsburgh, PA but I travel all over! Me and my gang love to have fun so, if you hate fun... don't book me. But if you wanna have the best time at your event, I got your back!

Originally from Atlanta, Georgia, I grew up immersed in Southern music traditions and moved to Pittsburgh in 2018 to pursue my musical career in the Steel City's thriving music scene. If you like artists like Morgan Wallen, Dylan Marlowe, and Dan + Shay, then you'll love our live show!

I gained significant local recognition by writing an authentic Pittsburgh country song in just 5 minutes during Y108's "Song on the Spot" radio segment. With my latest single "Cowboy Heartbreaker," I continue to build momentum as one of Pittsburgh's most exciting emerging country artists. My high-energy live performances and genuine connection with audiences have made me a favorite at venues and festivals throughout Pennsylvania and Ohio.

Performance & Booking Info: Available for weddings, corporate events, festivals, private parties, and more. Pricing: $400-$1,000 per event. Available for travel up to 200 miles from Pittsburgh.

I'm available for bookings, interviews, and media appearances. For more information, contact jackwildermanagement@gmail.com.
